## 🔴 Business Problem  

In the **highly competitive aviation industry**, passenger satisfaction is the key to loyalty and profitability.  
Airlines struggle with:  
- ❌ Identifying which service factors drive satisfaction (Wi‑Fi, seat comfort, delays)  
- ❌ Understanding how operational issues affect customer loyalty  
- ❌ Making data‑driven improvements to service quality  

➡️ Without insights, airlines risk **customer churn** and **lost revenue**.

---

## ⚙️ What I Did  


╔══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║   🧹 Data Cleaning   |   📊 Exploratory Analysis   |   🎨 Visualization ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝


- 🧹 **Data Cleaning** → Handled missing values, standardized categorical features  
- 📊 **Exploratory Analysis** → Grouped & aggregated survey responses with **Pandas**  
- 🎨 **Visualization** → Built colorful charts (satisfaction distribution, factor importance, delay impact)  
- 🧠 **Business Framing** → Linked findings to **service improvement strategies**  

---

## 📈 Results  

- ✅ Identified **top drivers of satisfaction**: seat comfort, inflight Wi‑Fi, and on‑time performance  
- ✅ Found that **flight delays strongly reduce satisfaction scores**  
- ✅ Showed that **loyalty programs & customer service** improve retention  
- ✅ Delivered **actionable recommendations** for service upgrades
